A capstone project of Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering 4th year
BERTO-Engineers

Marikina Polytechnic College faces a waste management issue, primarily affecting students. Researchers propose integrating BERTO-Bin, an IoT vending machine with an incentive system for solid waste recovery, to address this issue. The machine will replace used plastic bottles with rewards, encouraging community members to take responsibility for waste management.

The main objective of this study is to develop and determine the level of acceptability of respondents on establishing using the BERTO-Bin: IoT-Based Vending Machine with Incentive System for Solid Waste Recovery. The plastic bottle will be gathered to help the Marikina Polytechnic College lessen the waste with a reward.

Student. The study will provide participants with stationary and help to be disciplined in terms of environmental activities. Contribute to sustainable development and reduce the use of plastic trash, which allows healthy and risk free lives.
Administration. The school administration can support the researchers with their design project by initiating the adaptation and proceeding to the actual development plan of BERTO-Bin, considering its funding and its endorsement.


Scope


The study focused on the development and utilization of proper waste disposal with the use of innovations in technology. The researchers will use the Raspberry Pi as the primary unit and the sensor as the secondary unit. This study will be limited to the school year 2023-2024 at Marikina Polytechnic College

Limitations


The goal of this project is to assess and develop an integrated technology for waste management and stationary utilization as an incentive. The researchers will carry out the research on e-waste by replacing the plastic bottle with a rewarding point system of stationary generated by QR code. The method that the researchers will use is a quasi-experimental type of research that will evaluate the a).functionality, b). durability, c). sustainability, d). acceptability
